Youth Heart Society

A youth-led organization dedicated to social service since 2021.

About Us

Youth Heart Society is a voluntary youth organization established in 2021 with the aim of serving the community and empowering young people. We actively engage in social, environmental, educational, and humanitarian activities to create a positive impact in society.

Our Programs

  • 2021 – Blood Donation Program
  • 2022 – Environmental Cleaning Campaign
  • 2023 – Community Support & Awareness Programs

Our Mission

To inspire and mobilize youth to actively contribute to social development, promote volunteerism, and build a compassionate and responsible society.
